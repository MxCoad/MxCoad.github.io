Trash pixels, texture seams and artifacts

When you run your game you notice there are extra pixels being rendered on the sprites.

Example:

This can happen due to the way the atlas is storing the image data and the way it is rendered. To fix the probelm, increase the 'Extrude Borders' property in the atlas. Try the value 2 to see if that works, and increase further if necessary.
